Satoru Iwata

4th President and CEO of Nintendo

"On my business card, I am a corporate president. In my mind, I am a game developer. But in my heart, I am a gamer."

From humble beginnings as a part-time programmer for HAL Laboratory, Iwata's skill and passion launched him into several positions of repute before landing him as the CEO of Nintendo.

Credited for reviving and reshaping the company in a time of public disinterest, Iwata's contributions to Nintendo, gaming, and family entertainment as a whole cannot be overstated.
